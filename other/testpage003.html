Halo Reach Help

Filed under: Halo Reach, Uncategorized — Leave a comment
December 18, 2010

Hello if you need help with Halo Reach achievements add me to your friends on the Xbox 360. My gamer tag is masterdaddy117

Here are some tips to get achievements:
1. Get a friend to help
2. Work as a team
3. Make a plan with strategy and tactics.

For example I got my brother and dad to help and they became my team. Our strategic plan was to all ways stick together and aim at the same enemy for example an Elite. This is needed because an Elite will  form a team of 2 or 3 Elites with lower ranking Grunts occasionally following.  The tactics is to assign each team member a target for example, my Dad would would aim at grunts and my brother and I  aim at the Elites.
